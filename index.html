Good to see you!

Welcome to Elemental Theory, a platform to promote LAVA (Live-Action Vocal Acting) Games, a new genre of interactive fantasy! My name is Robby Howell, and I have been working to develop and introduce LAVA Games to the world for almost a decade.

If you  are interested in playing, I greatly look forward to hearing from you! Remember that there are several different LAVA Games available for play at this time (specifically Enclave, Lifetime, and Sacred Path), so I would suggest you check out all three to figure out which you would prefer.

In addition to my work with LAVA, I also offer my services as a teacher, lecturer, and workshop leader, applying what I have learned through my games to a wide variety of topics. Depending on the event, I am happy either to incorporate LAVA or keep to more standard fare.

So thank you for stopping by Elemental Theory! As we start to bring LAVA more into the public eye there will be plenty​ of new and exciting things happening with us, so please do keep an eye out to see what happens next! Please contact me at any time with questions.

Ciao for now!
